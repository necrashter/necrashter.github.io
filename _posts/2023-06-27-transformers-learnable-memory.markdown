In this semester, we implemented the paper called ["Fine-tuning Image Transformers using Learnable Memory"](https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.15243) for [CENG 502 Advanced Deep Learning](https://user.ceng.metu.edu.tr/~skalkan/ADL/) course.
The paper was published in [CVPR 2022](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2022/html/Sandler_Fine-Tuning_Image_Transformers_Using_Learnable_Memory_CVPR_2022_paper.html) but it had no open-source implementation before our project to the best of our knowledge.

Related links for our project:
- [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/necrashter/transformers-learnable-memory) of the code. MIT License.
- [HuggingFace Repository](https://huggingface.co/necrashter/transformers-learnable-memory) containing the models we trained.
